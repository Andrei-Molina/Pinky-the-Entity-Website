Prologue: The Cosmic Divergence

In a realm where the gods once ruled with absolute power, a being known as Pinky emerged from the cosmic chaos. Born from the remnants of divine conflict, Pinky was neither god nor mortal, but something entirely different—a force of nature that defied the very fabric of reality.

As Pinky traversed the universe, it encountered various realms, each shaped by the emotions and desires of their inhabitants. From the serene Still Fields to the tumultuous Plains of Envy, Pinky's journey was marked by encounters with beings who embodied the seven deadly sins.

Each chapter of Pinky's story reveals a new aspect of existence, exploring themes of power, freedom, and the consequences of unchecked ambition. Through trials and tribulations, Pinky learns that true strength lies not in domination but in understanding and compassion.

The final chapter culminates in a confrontation with the gods themselves, where Pinky must choose between vengeance and redemption. In a world where knowledge is both a gift and a curse, Pinky's ultimate decision will shape the fate of all realms.
