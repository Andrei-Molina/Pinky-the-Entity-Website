Pinky The Entity

The main character of Pinky The Entity, Pinky is a curious and adventurous slimian from the planet Slimoroid. Small in size but full of determination, he has the ability to stretch, bounce, and shift his form in ways that help him survive dangerous worlds.

Despite his cheerful appearance, Pinky is no ordinary slime—he carries a mysterious energy within him, one that attracts both powerful allies and relentless enemies. On his journey, Pinky seeks not only to explore strange lands but also to uncover the truth behind his origins and the secret of the “Entity” that dwells inside him.

Brave, mischievous, and endlessly resilient, Pinky is the heart of the adventure—whether leaping across platforms, outsmarting rivals, or protecting those he cares about.

The All-Seeing Eye

Known only as The All-Seeing Eye, this strange being seems to know far more than it should. Its unblinking gaze unsettles Pinky, for it speaks of secrets—about the world, about the Entity, even about Pinky himself—that he has never known.

Pinky first found it trapped in a force field after awakening from a long slumber. Yet even in captivity, the Eye did not beg for freedom—it only watched, as if it had been waiting for him all along.

Ominous and cryptic, the Eye may be a guide… or something far more dangerous.

Blocky The Armless

Blocky the Armless is a strange figure who awoke in an unknown place with only his legs to carry him. His arms are missing, though whether by fate, design, or something darker, he cannot say.

Despite his odd form, Blocky is friendly and harmless—or so Pinky believes. His tough, block-like shell makes him surprisingly resilient, able to leap great heights and smash through obstacles without so much as a scratch.

Though his past is a mystery, Blocky’s determination and disarming nature make him an unlikely but steadfast companion in Pinky’s journey.

Robotri the Robot

Robotri the Robo was born of cosmic craftsmanship, yet condemned as a failure. Cast adrift into the endless void, he wandered for millennia—silent, broken, and forgotten among the stars.

When Pinky found him in a barren wasteland, Robotri was little more than a husk. Only through Pinky’s mysterious power did his fading spark flicker back to life, though many of his parts remain shattered and incomplete.

Haunted by rejection and the emptiness of his long exile, Robotri follows Pinky with quiet resolve— not out of duty alone, but in the faint hope that their journey may reveal the purpose he was never meant to have.

Green: The Envy

Born from a fragment of Pinky’s soul during the cataclysm of the Arbitral of the Court of Cosmic Divergence, Green: The Envy is both a reflection of Pinky and his twisted opposite. He embodies the restless hunger of envy, always yearning for what others have and never content with his own form.

His power is as dangerous as it is insidious—he can mimic the abilities of those he faces, or twist their hearts with envy until allies turn bitter and foes grow reckless. Yet the more Pinky grows, the more Green feeds, drawing strength from what was once his other half.

Jealous, cunning, and unyielding, Green is a constant shadow to Pinky’s light—a reminder that even the brightest soul carries darkness within.

The Amber: Greed

One of the first fragments torn from Pinky’s soul, Amber: The Greed embodies the insatiable urge to take and never let go. What began as Pinky’s desire to hold on to what he loved became twisted into obsession—an endless craving to claim all things for himself.

His power thrives on possession. Amber can drain the strength of others, stealing abilities and hoarding them within his core. Every victory, every treasure, every ounce of power he gathers is guarded jealously, as though the very act of owning defines his existence.

Covetous, manipulative, and unrelenting, Amber stands as a shadow of Pinky’s soul—a collector who will never be satisfied, no matter how much he takes.

Guu Lutonny

Born from one of the lesser shards of Pinky’s soul, Guu Lutonny is the embodiment of gluttony. Unlike his stronger counterparts, he carries no desire for conquest or rivalry—only the endless urge to eat, to consume, and to fill the emptiness within.

Though weak in power and timid in nature, his hunger itself is relentless. Guu can absorb almost anything he devours, breaking it down to sustain himself or twisting it into strange bursts of strength. Yet his satisfaction never lasts, for the moment his mouth is empty, the craving returns.

Simple, harmless, and ever-hungry, Guu Lutonny is a proof that even small desires can grow into endless voids if left unchecked.

Lust Arcadia

Once counted among the most notorious fragments of Pinky’s soul, Lust Arcadia has vanished without a trace. The other fragments speak of him with awe, recalling a figure whose presence was as commanding as it was unsettling. His absence lingers like a shadow, driving the rest to search for him relentlessly.

Lust Arcadia’s form bears a striking resemblance to Pinky, his body glowing with the same vibrant pink. This likeness led many to mistake Pinky for him, until battle revealed the truth. Where Pinky carries innocence, Arcadia carries desire, a force that bends and binds those who fall under its sway.

Alluring, dangerous, and unseen, Lust Arcadia remains a mystery. Whether his return will unite or destroy the fragments is a question none can answer.

Rath

Among the most feared fragments of Pinky’s soul, Rath is the living embodiment of rage. Always seething, always on the edge of eruption, he believes that every problem can be crushed with force. His wrath is unending, and his fists have shaped both ruin and resolution.

In one legendary outburst, his overwhelming power shattered two rival kingdoms into submission, forcing them into peace through sheer fear. Yet beneath the chaos lies a fierce code—Rath holds deep respect for Lust Arcadia, and any insult toward the missing fragment is enough to awaken his fury.

Unstoppable, volatile, and brutally honest, Rath stands as one of the top ten forces in cosmic space you do not want to provoke.

Aergia: The Idle

Among the strangest fragments of Pinky’s soul, Aergia: The Idle was bestowed with the power to remain still and yet endure. Though capable of immense strength, he hides it behind endless slumber, choosing to live in quiet idleness rather than reveal the force he possesses.

Known as one of the strongest entities in cosmic space, Aergia’s days are spent drifting into sleep, whether beneath a tree, within a cavern, or even in the heart of a volcano. His apathy shields him from conflict, for he simply does not care to move unless disturbed.

Only one thing can rouse him from his lethargy: the insult of Lust Arcadia’s name. To speak ill of Arcadia is to awaken Aergia’s fury, a rare event feared across the stars.

Vania The Prideful

Among the most regal of Pinky’s fragments, Vania: The Prideful carries himself as though he were a king stripped of his throne. Noble in bearing yet dangerous in nature, he believes his rightful place is above all others, and his pride will not allow him to bow before anyone.

His strength rises with his conviction, each battle feeding his belief that he alone was destined to rule. Though many have sought to humble him, none have succeeded, for his will hardens in the face of challenge and his pride becomes an unbreakable shield.

Majestic, relentless, and untamed, Vania remains a towering force among the fragments—a fallen king who refuses to accept his fall, and whose pride may yet shake the stars.
